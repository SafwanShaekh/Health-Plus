Oral Health Precautions & Treatments

Many things we mindlessly do day in and out play a large role in our dental health. We may smoke or consume sugary foods and drinks throughout the day. These habits have different consequences throughout the whole body, but we will focus on how they affect the dentition here.

  • Eat a healthy diet.
  • Consume less salt and sugar.
  • Reduce intake of harmful fats.
  • Check your blood pressure regularly.
